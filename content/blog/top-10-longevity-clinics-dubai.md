Dubai has positioned itself as one of the world's leading destinations for longevity medicine. The combination of progressive healthcare regulation, tax-free economics, and a health-conscious population has attracted world-class clinics from Switzerland, Israel, and beyond. Whether you are looking for a comprehensive multi-week longevity program or a single session of [NAD+ IV therapy](/treatments/nad-injectable), the city offers options at every level.

This guide reviews the top 10 longevity clinics in Dubai — what they specialize in, what they charge, and who they are best suited for.

## What to Look for in a Longevity Clinic

Before choosing a clinic, consider these factors:

- **Diagnostic depth** — The best clinics start with comprehensive testing: [blood panels](/treatments/hormone-panel-testing) (50-100+ biomarkers), [whole-body MRI](/treatments/whole-body-mri), [DEXA scans](/treatments/dexa-scan), and [biological age testing](/treatments/biological-age-testing). Avoid clinics that prescribe treatments without baseline data.
- **Qualified practitioners** — Board-certified physicians with longevity or regenerative medicine training. Ask about credentials.
- **Personalized protocols** — One-size-fits-all packages are a red flag. Your protocol should be based on your test results and health goals.
- **Follow-up monitoring** — Regular biomarker retesting (every 8-12 weeks) to track whether interventions are working.
- **Evidence-based approach** — Clinics should be transparent about the evidence level for each treatment they recommend.

## The Top 10 Longevity Clinics in Dubai

The arrival of Clinique La Prairie marked a turning point for Dubai's longevity scene. This Swiss institution brings over 90 years of longevity expertise to a 3,800-square-meter hub with 29 treatment rooms.

**Specialization:** Comprehensive anti-aging and longevity programs. Their proprietary Longevity Index combines advanced diagnostics, regenerative therapies, nutrition, and holistic wellness into structured multi-day programs.

**Key treatments:** [Biological age testing](/treatments/biological-age-testing), epigenetic analysis, [stem cell therapy](/treatments/stem-cell-therapy), [IV therapy](/treatments/iv-drip-therapy), nutritional optimization, sleep protocols, and aesthetic regeneration.

**Pricing:** Programs start from approximately AED 15,000 for shorter assessments and can exceed AED 50,000 for comprehensive multi-day protocols.

**Best for:** Those seeking a premium, medically supervised longevity program from an internationally recognized institution. The price point reflects the depth of diagnostics and the Swiss clinical methodology.

### 2. Aviv Clinics — JLT

Their protocols are based on peer-reviewed research from Tel Aviv University led by Professor Shai Efrati, whose studies demonstrated measurable telomere lengthening and senescent cell reduction in human participants.

**Specialization:** Brain health, cognitive performance, and evidence-based age reversal through [hyperbaric oxygen therapy](/treatments/hyperbaric-oxygen).

**Key treatments:** The Aviv Medical Program combines 40-60 HBOT sessions over 8-12 weeks with cognitive training, physical conditioning, and nutritional guidance. Comprehensive brain MRI, neurocognitive assessments, and biomarker testing before and after.

**Pricing:** Full Aviv Medical Program: AED 55,000-75,000. Individual HBOT sessions: AED 1,000-1,500.

**Best for:** Those serious about cognitive enhancement, brain injury recovery, or evidence-based biological age reversal. This is a medical program, not a casual wellness session.

## What to Expect at Your First Visit

Most longevity clinics follow a similar initial process:

1. **Health history review** — A detailed questionnaire covering medical history, family history, lifestyle, current medications, and health goals.
2. **Comprehensive blood work** — Advanced panels testing 50-100+ biomarkers including [hormones](/treatments/hormone-panel-testing) (testosterone, estradiol, DHEA-S, thyroid), inflammatory markers (hs-CRP, homocysteine), metabolic markers (fasting insulin, HbA1c), cardiovascular markers (ApoB, Lp(a)), and nutrient levels.
3. **Body composition analysis** — [DEXA scan](/treatments/dexa-scan) for bone density, lean mass, and fat distribution. Some clinics include VO2 max testing.
4. **Advanced imaging** — Premium clinics may include [whole-body MRI](/treatments/whole-body-mri) for early detection of abnormalities.
5. **Consultation** — Review of results with a physician, discussion of your health goals, and recommendation of a personalized protocol.
6. **Ongoing monitoring** — Biomarker retesting every 8-12 weeks to track progress and adjust protocols.

## How to Choose the Right Clinic

**Start with your goal:**
- **Brain health and cognitive performance** → Aviv Clinics
- **Comprehensive anti-aging** → Clinique La Prairie or AEON Clinic
- **Diagnostics and baseline testing** → ZOIME or DNA Health & Wellness
- **Root-cause health investigation** → Advanced Health Dubai
- **Regular recovery and optimization** → Formation or UCRYO
- **Medical-grade treatments with clinical oversight** → Wellth by Medcare

**Consider your budget:**
- **Under AED 500/month** → Formation or UCRYO memberships for regular cryotherapy, infrared sauna, and red light therapy
- **AED 1,000-5,000 one-time** → Comprehensive diagnostics at ZOIME or DNA Health
- **AED 5,000-20,000** → Multi-session NAD+ or HBOT protocols at AEON or Wellth
- **AED 20,000+** → Full longevity programs at Clinique La Prairie, Aviv, or Bioscience Institute

**Start small if you are unsure.** Book a comprehensive blood panel at one clinic, get your baseline numbers, and then decide which treatments make sense for your specific biology. Spending AED 50,000 on a longevity program without first understanding your starting point is a common and avoidable mistake.
