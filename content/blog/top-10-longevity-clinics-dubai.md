## What is Longevity Medicine?

Longevity medicine focuses on extending healthspan — the number of years you live in good health. Rather than treating disease after it appears, longevity clinics use advanced diagnostics, biomarker tracking, and cutting-edge therapies to prevent decline and optimize performance.

## What to Look for in a Longevity Clinic

When choosing a longevity clinic, consider:

- **Comprehensive diagnostics** — Full-body MRI, advanced blood panels, genetic testing
- **Personalized protocols** — Treatments tailored to your unique biology
- **Qualified practitioners** — Board-certified doctors with longevity specialization
- **Evidence-based approach** — Treatments backed by clinical research
- **Follow-up care** — Ongoing monitoring and protocol adjustments

## Common Treatments Offered

Dubai's top longevity clinics typically offer:

- **NAD+ IV Therapy** — Supports cellular energy and repair
